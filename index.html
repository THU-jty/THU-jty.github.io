About me

My name is Tianyang Jiang (姜天洋). I am currently a forth-year PhD student at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Guangyan Zhang. Before that, I received my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2018.

My research interests span distributed systems, storage and transaction processing.

Research

Cloud tenants demand more on the quality of service nowadays in cloud storage systems. My goal is to achieve the service-level agreement in each tier of cloud storage hierachy, including consistent latency/bandwidth and low abort rate. So my work lie in distributed systems, in-memory systems, storage systems.

Here are some of my projects:

  • Achieving consistent low latency in all-flash arrays: FusionRAID (FAST'21)
  • Alleviating aborts for in-memory distributed transactions: Aurogon (FAST'22)
